<h2>Introduction to Financial Accounting</h2>
<p>Course introduces process of recording, summarizing, and reporting the transactions resulting from business operations over a period of time. An introductory financial accounting course teaches the fundamentals of this process, including how to read, analyze, and prepare the three main financial statements: the income statement, the balance sheet, and the statement of cash flows. Course also teaches the  core principles and concepts that underlie financial accounting, such as the double-entry bookkeeping system, and how to apply them to real-world business scenarios. The course also covers topics like revenue recognition, inventory management, and long-term asset accounting, equipping students with the skills to understand how companies manage their finances and communicate their performance to external stakeholders.</p>
